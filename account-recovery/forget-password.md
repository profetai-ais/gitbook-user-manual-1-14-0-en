# Forgot Password

## Reset Password When Mail Server Is Configured <a href="#yi-she-ding-mail-server-shi-zhong-she-mi-ma" id="yi-she-ding-mail-server-shi-zhong-she-mi-ma"></a>

When the system has a Mail Server configured, users can reset their password on their own via a password reset email.​

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (288).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (289).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (290).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (291).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page.
2. Enter your account information.
3. Click **Retrieve Password**.
4. The system will send a password reset email to the email address linked to your account. Open the email and click the password reset button inside.
5. The system will redirect you to the password reset page. Enter and confirm your new password.
6. Once your password has been updated, return to the login page and sign in with your new password.

> Note: Please make sure the account information you enter matches your registered account. If you do not receive the password reset email, check your spam or promotions folder, or contact your administrator for assistance.

​

## Reset Password When Mail Server Is Not Configured <a href="#wei-she-ding-mail-server-shi-zhong-she-mi-ma" id="wei-she-ding-mail-server-shi-zhong-she-mi-ma"></a>

When the system does not have a Mail Server configured, the system cannot send a password reset email. Users must contact the administrator to request a password reset.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (288).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (292).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (293).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (294).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (295).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page.
2. The system will display a prompt informing you that you need to contact the administrator to reset your password.
3. Contact your administrator directly and provide the account information for which you need the password reset. The administrator will provide you with a temporary password.
4. Return to the login page and enter your account and temporary password.
5. After logging in with the temporary password, the system will redirect you to the password reset page. Enter and confirm your new password.
6. Once your password has been updated, use your new password to log in going forward.

> Note: For security reasons, please complete the password reset immediately after logging in with the temporary password. Do not share the temporary password or your new password with anyone.

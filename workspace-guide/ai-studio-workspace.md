## **Explore Agents and Add to Favorites**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (394).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Click "Workspace" in the left sidebar menu
2. Hover over the Agent you want to use, or enter keywords in the search bar to find an Agent
3. After selecting an Agent, click the button on the card to add it to your personal favorites
4. Click the "Favorites" button at the top of the page to switch to the personal area and verify the Agent has been added

## **View Agents in Personal Favorites**&#x20;

Click the "Favorites" button at the top of the page to switch to the personal area and view your saved Agents or application templates. Click the menu button in the upper-right corner of an Agent / application template card, then click the heart button again to remove the selected Agent / application template from your personal favorites.
